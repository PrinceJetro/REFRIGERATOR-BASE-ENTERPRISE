REFRIGERATOR BASE ENTERPRISE

REFRIGERATOR BASE ENTERPRISE is a Greek company based in Lagos, Nigeria. We specialize in marine refrigeration and air conditioning systems, parts, and technical support for vessels worldwide.

Our core offering revolves around the global sourcing, procurement, and worldwide delivery of marine refrigeration spare parts, products, and accessories. We leverage an extensive database and a spare parts center staff who work with all major marine refrigeration Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).

"We can source, procure and arrange the delivery of marine refrigeration spare parts, products and accessories worldwide to your vessels or company."

Our spare parts centre staff deal with all the major marine refrigeration original equipment manufacturers, and using our extensive database we can locate and deliver the very important items to your vessels or company. We also give support for compressor and spare part for your system.

Our Expertise

Specialized knowledge across all marine cooling and refrigeration systems

Marine Refrigeration

Complete refrigeration systems for fishing vessels, cargo ships, and passenger liners. Specializing in low-temperature applications for fish holds and cargo refrigeration.

  • Fish hold refrigeration systems
  • Cargo refrigeration units
  • Provision cooling systems
  • Ice-making machines

Industrial Refrigeration

Heavy-duty refrigeration solutions for processing plants, cold storage facilities, and industrial applications requiring precise temperature control.

  • Ammonia refrigeration systems
  • Cold storage solutions
  • Process cooling systems
  • Blast freezing equipment

Air Dryers

Compressed air drying systems essential for marine applications, preventing moisture damage to pneumatic systems and instrumentation.

  • Refrigerated air dryers
  • Desiccant air dryers
  • Membrane air dryers
  • Heatless regeneration dryers

Air Conditioning Systems

Marine HVAC systems for crew comfort and equipment cooling, including specialized systems for engine rooms and electronic equipment.

  • Cabin air conditioning
  • Engine room cooling
  • Electronics cooling systems
  • Central AC systems
